Radha Krishna

Author:Esaiah Udhayakumar

"Radha-Krishna: Sacred Love Stories" explore the divine love between Radha and Krishna through a series of narratives, some based on scripture and others on fiction. It's a narrative rich in mythology and symbolism, often interpreted differently depending on individual traditions and beliefs. Radha and Krishna represent avatars of the two supreme deities: Vishnu and his partner Lakshmi.
